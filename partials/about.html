"A polyomino is a plane geometric figure formed by joining one or more equal squares edge to edge. It is a polyform whose cells are squares." Wikipedia

Simply put...have you ever played Tetris? Those are polyominoes. This page deals with what are referred to as "one-sided" polyominoes, which effectively means that the reflection of a shape can create a new shape as long as that 'new' shape cannot be achieved by rotating the object.
